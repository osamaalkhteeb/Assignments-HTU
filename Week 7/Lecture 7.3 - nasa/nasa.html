NASA’s SpaceX Crew-10

The agency’s SpaceX Crew-10 mission launched to the International Space Station at 7:03 p.m. EDT Friday, March 14, from Launch Complex 39A at Kennedy Space Center in Florida. The spacecraft docked to the orbiting complex at 12:04 a.m. EDT on Sunday. March 16, while the station was roughly 260 statute miles over Altantic Ocean.
